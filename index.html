OUR MANIFESTO

Destined for more
than just average.

You are in a world full of noise. Everyone else is shouting loudly at the top of their lungs, so your first instinct is to yell back, right?

Well, bad news... you cannot out-shout them. And quite frankly, we don't think you should even try.

The loudest person in the room isn't the best; they are just... well... loud. We believe in another path.

Shyuzz Studio is here to help you disrupt, leaving your competitors speechless. Because the world doesn't need another echo - it needs a voice.

We design graphics, branding campaigns, and murals that capture attention, build deep visual tension, and hold it. No templates, no average layouts. Only raw creative strategy.

Selected Case Studies

Exhibition Design • Branding

“Can You See The Beauty”

Comprehensive exhibition design and identity for artist Gil Goren at the Hermann Struck Museum. The layout creates a bold, RGB-based contemporary dialogue with the museum's historical architecture.

Branding • Transport Design

Haifa Tourism: The Red Bus

Visual concept and detailed graphic design mapping for Haifa's official tourism double-decker bus, adding character and vibrant local identity to the streetscape.

Branding • Self-Identity

Branding Myself: The Difficult Client

Developing a cheeky, bold, and sassy visual identity for Shyuzz Studio. Infused with neon hues, custom icons, and interactive digital layout concepts.

Environmental Art • Mural Design

Garin Tzabar: Community Wall Art

A large-scale public mural design created for Garin Tzabar, blending illustrative community symbols with bold graphic elements to revitalize a shared urban space.

Mural • Commercial Spaces

Papa Bakery Murals

Handcrafted interior mural designs created for Papa Bakery, establishing a warm, artisanal brand story through typographic painting and illustrations.

Identity • Event Branding

Pop Art Family Festival

Visual language, logo, and marketing banner suites developed for the Pop Art Family Festival, featuring high-contrast illustration styling.

Campaign • Editorial Poster

Haifa Museums: Campaign Series

A series of high-energy exhibition marketing posters and outdoor banners designed for Haifa Museums, using bold colorblocking and structured typography to maximize public engagement.
